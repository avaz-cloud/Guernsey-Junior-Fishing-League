GETTING INVOLVED

Learn How It Works

No entry forms, no fees — just turn up, fish fair, and log your catch.

Sign up free

Register once to get your league membership card. No cost, no catch — just your name on the league.

Fish the window

Catches count from 4pm Friday to 9pm Sunday, every weekend, and throughout school holiday periods, from anywhere around Guernsey's coastline.

Log your catch

Submit your species and measurement/weight — solo or as part of your group — before the window closes.

Watch the board

Results are posted after each competition, with winners called out across every prize category.

OUR STORY

Started by a 12-year-old with a rod and a group chat.

The Guernsey Junior Fishing League began the way most good ideas do — with a few friends heading down to the coast, comparing catches, and wondering who'd landed the biggest fish that weekend. Word spread from friend to friend, and what started as a small group has grown into an island-wide league of young anglers.

Every competition is entirely free to enter and run by the juniors themselves — no adults calling the shots, just young anglers organising fair, friendly competition for their own community. Whether you're chasing a personal best or just want an excuse to get outdoors with your mates, there's a place for you on the rocks.

"At the start, I just reached out to a few friends. They told their friends, and it grew from there." — Herbie Zunino, Founder, age 12

Sponsors

The Guernsey Junior Fishing League is always looking for sponsors. Every pound raised through sponsorship and donations is reinvested straight back into the league — most of it goes towards prizes for our members.

Prizes aren't only awarded to whoever catches the biggest or best fish. We also recognise members who show enthusiasm, commitment, great participation, and a positive attitude — because that's what keeps this league going.
