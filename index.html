OUR STORY

Started by a 12-year-old with a rod and a group chat.

The Guernsey Junior Fishing League began the way most good ideas do — with a few friends heading down to the coast, comparing catches, and wondering who'd landed the biggest fish that weekend. Word spread from friend to friend, and what started as a small group has grown into an island-wide league of young anglers.

Every competition is entirely free to enter and run by the juniors themselves — no adults calling the shots, just young anglers organising fair, friendly competition for their own community. Whether you're chasing a personal best or just want an excuse to get outdoors with your mates, there's a place for you on the rocks.

"At the start, I just reached out to a few friends. They told their friends, and it grew from there." — Herbie Zunino, Founder, age 12

WHAT'S ON OFFER

Everything you need, nothing you pay for.

From your first entry to your first trophy, here's what being part of the league gets you.

Free Sea Competitions

Regular junior sea fishing competitions held almost every weekend around the island — always free to enter, always open to new members.

FRI 4PM – SUN 9PM

Group Entries

Fish solo or gather your mates into a group — enter together, weigh in together, and see how your crew stacks up on the league table.

ANY GROUP SIZE

Official Membership Cards

Every registered member receives a league membership card, marking you as an official part of the Guernsey Junior Fishing League.

FREE ON SIGN-UP

Prizes For Every Category

Win by species, by size, by group, or by most-improved catch. Prizes are spread across categories so everyone has something to aim for.

MULTIPLE CATEGORIES

Results Every Weekend

Catches from anywhere around the island between Friday afternoon and Sunday evening are logged and posted to the league board.

ISLAND-WIDE

A Community, Not Just A League

Run by juniors, for juniors — a friendly space to swap tips, share your best catches and make new fishing mates along the coastline.

GETTING INVOLVED

How a weekend competition works

No entry forms, no fees — just turn up, fish fair, and log your catch.

Sign up free

Register once to get your league membership card. No cost, no catch — just your name on the roster.

Fish the window

Catches count from 4pm Friday to 9pm Sunday, from anywhere around Guernsey's coastline.

Log your catch

Submit your species and measurement — solo or as part of your group — before the window closes.

Watch the board

Results are posted after each competition, with winners called out across every prize category.
